Our Vision

To create a Learning Management System that gets out of the way and allows students and instructors to spend 100% of the class subject and 0% on figuring up the LMS.

Our Method

[Disclaimer: This is a list of goals for the system, some of these features are under development]

For Students

  • Data-Driven: We use data to show likely scenarios from where you are, and you can use that to make informed decisions.
  • Equitable: Every student can complete the work, for some, it will take less time and for others more, but everyone should be able to complete it.
  • Transparent: All grades are transparent and can be improved upon.

For Instructors

  • Check-Listed: Your courses follow the standards listed below.
  • Data-Driven: Get access to how your class is doing and recommendations on possible actions to take. Check-Listed: Your courses follow the standards listed below.
  • Off-the-shelf Content: Use pre-designed content for your class so you don’t have to re-create materials that already exist.
  • Customized Content (optional): Create your own content to add your flavor to the class and if interested shared that content with others.

For Both

  • Minimalistic: You shouldn’t need to learn the way to study how the LMS works.
